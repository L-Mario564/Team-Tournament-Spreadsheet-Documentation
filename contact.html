Contact

Want to contact me to report a bug, suggest a feature, or contribute to the spreadsheet or the documentation website? This is the place to do so. I'd like to keep improving and fixing the project as much as I can.

You're free to contact me as long as you follow these rules:

  • You must have a valid reason to contact me. I won't take suggestions relating to visuals or code formatting.
  • You must describe your report with detail. I can't help you if your report is vague.
  • If your report is about the spreadsheet, give information about the sheets, scripts and / or ranges that are related to your issue.
  • If you're suggesting a feature, make sure it isn't already in place.
  • If you're suggesting a feature, you must describe how it would improve the project.
